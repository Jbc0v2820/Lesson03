Responsibilties

Reference

Assist NCGA members, staff and the general public with reference questions and research requests. Used PACER system to track court cases and provide electronic court documents to Senate Leadership staff, Legislators and Research Division staff.

Training/Teaching

Develop and conduct group classes (NCGA Bill Inquiry System, NCGA website and the Library webpage) and one-on-one training for new employees, interns/externs.

Westlaw

Manage NCGA Westlaw program - issuing passwords for new employees, scheduling group and one-on-one training as needed for all Westlaw users and troubleshooting problems for all Westlaw users.

Webpage Design & Maintenance

Redesign and manage Library and Legislative Analysis Division webpage. Major redesign in 2008 and in 2019. Most recently, the redesign involved converting the webpages from Dreamweaver to WordPress.

Publications

Create and maintain many library marketing materials (such as Library brochure and bookmark) and useful reference tools (demographic, statistical and historical materials) for both print and the webpage. Examples of reference documents include: African Americans in the General Assembly, Women in the General Assembly, Budget History 1961-2019, Legislative Statistics 1965-2019, Amendments to the NC Constitution of 1971, and North Carolina Ratification of U.S. Constitutional Amendments. In addition, design Library and NCGA print publications such as posters, brochures, maps and guides and take beautiful photographs for the Library webpage.
